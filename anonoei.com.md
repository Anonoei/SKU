## C: Clothing 
Pattern: [C-<`Sex`><`Conditions`>-<`Category`><`Type`><`Subtype (0)`>-<`(R##)`><`(D##)`>]

Example: C-UC-TH0-R01D01
 - U: Unisex
 - F: Female
 - M: Male
   - H: Hot weather
   - C: Cold weather
   - A: Any weather
     - H: Headwear
       - H: Hats
       - S: Scarves
       - M: Masks
     - T: Tops
       - B: Blouse
       - C: Crop Top
       - T: T-shirt
       - L: Long-sleeve shirt
       - S: Sweater
       - H: Hoodie
         - Z: Zip-up
       - A: Tank Top
     - B: Bottoms
       - P: Pants
       - S: Shorts
       - L: Leggings
         - S: Spandex
     - F: Footwear
     - A: Activewear

## A: Accessories
Pattern: [A-<`Category`><`Type`><`Subtype (0)`>-<`(R##)`><`(D##)`>]

Example: A-DM0-R01D01
 - H: Home
 - D: Desk
   - M: Mousepad
 - W: Wear
 - T: Transport
   - L: Laptop Sleeve
   - P: Phone case
 - O: Other
   - S: Stickers
     - H: Holographic
